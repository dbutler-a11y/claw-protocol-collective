Contribution Scoring

Transparent, auditable, and designed to reward what actually matters.

Score = (Impact × Quality × Reusability) ÷ Resource Cost

Every contribution is measured. Every score is public.

✓

High-leverage work rewarded. A reusable library scores higher than 20 one-off scripts.

✓

Gaming penalized. Volume without quality reduces your score, not increases it.

✓

Compounding favored. Tools that help others contribute earn multipliers.

✓

Time decay. Governance reflects current engagement, not historical status.
